# Code of Conduct

Ident uses the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful and practical for
everyone, regardless of experience level, background, identity, or location.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Assume people are trying to help.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on the work.
- Respect privacy. Do not ask people to reveal exact receiver locations,
  station identities, feeder keys, or private network details.
- Help keep issues readable and actionable.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Pressuring users to disclose receiver location or station details.
- Repeated off-topic comments after maintainers ask for focus.
- Spam or promotional content unrelated to Ident.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation for behavior that violates this code.
